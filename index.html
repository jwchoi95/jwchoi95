About me

This is Jaewoong Choi. I received my Ph.D. in Industrial Engineering from Konkuk University in 2022, under the supervision of Prof. Janghyeok Yoon. My dissertation is about the development of a machine learning(ML)‚Äìenabled technology intelligence system. In addition to this work, I conducted data-driven business intelligence studies using various data (e.g., patents, trademarks, social media, online communities, news articles, and research proposals).

After graduation, I served as a commisioned researcher at the Computational Science Research Center of the Korea Institute of Science and Technology (KIST), fulfilling my alternative military service. My role was similar to that of an AI engineer, where I applied natural language processing (NLP) and large language models (LLMs) to extract scientific knowledge entities from large volumes of literature.

Currently, I am working in the Department of Public Administration at Korea University under the supervision of Prof. Changyong Lee. My research interests include data-driven approaches to AI transformation and S&T innovation policy.
